ABOUT MediCare

MediCare Annaba is a multi-specialty hospital located in the heart of Annaba. Our state-of-the-art facility provides a wide range of diagnostics and therapeutic services at an affordable cost, all delivered with a patient-first, ethical approach.

  • Comprehensive treatment options
  • Cutting-edge technology and equipment
  • Award-winning quality of care
  • 24/7 ambulance support
  • Renowned and experienced medical staff

“At MediCare Annaba, our commitment is to elevate healthcare standards by combining expertise with compassion.”

CEO, Dr. Amina Bensaïd

Vision

To be the leading healthcare provider in Annaba, committed to wellness and innovation.

Mission

Deliver accessible, compassionate, and high-quality care through professional excellence and ethical practice.

Core Values

Integrity, Compassion, Teamwork, Responsibility, and Excellence.

OUR DEPARTMENTS

MediCare Annaba spans an 8,000 m² campus with 320 beds, serving over 35 specialties.

Cardiology

Expert cardiologists providing advanced diagnostics and interventional treatments.

Dermatology & Cosmetology

Comprehensive skin care services, from clinical treatments to aesthetic procedures.

Emergency & ICU

24/7 critical care with dedicated emergency, CCU, and ICU teams.

General Surgery

Experienced surgeons performing complex and minimally invasive procedures.

Health Checkup Packages

Affordable comprehensive health screening packages for early detection.

Neurology

Advanced care by a multidisciplinary team of neurologists, neurosurgeons, and therapists.

At MediCare Annaba, quality healthcare is our promise.

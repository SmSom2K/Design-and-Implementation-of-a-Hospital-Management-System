Our Specialities

MediCare Annaba offers a full range of specialized medical services with cutting-edge technology, all at an affordable cost. Our patient-centered approach ensures ethical, high-quality care across every department.

24/7 Ambulance Service

Emergency ambulance support around the clock, with fully equipped vehicles and trained staff.

LASIK Vision Correction

State-of-the-art LASIK procedures to correct vision with precision and safety.

Stroke Care Center

Dedicated stroke unit with rapid response protocols and advanced treatment options.

High-Tech Operating Theaters

Fifteen operating rooms equipped with the latest surgical technology for optimal outcomes.

Joint Replacement Center

Expert care in knee and hip replacement using minimally invasive techniques.

Endoscopy Suites

Two fully equipped suites for both digestive and respiratory endoscopic procedures.
